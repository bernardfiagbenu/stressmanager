Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Data Collection

Mindful Break operates entirely in your browser and does not store any personal data. All break preferences and activities are processed locally on your device. We do not collect, share, or sell any user information.

Local Storage

Any preferences you set during your session are stored locally in your browser. This data is never transmitted to our servers and is automatically cleared when you close your browser.

Third-Party Services

We use Font Awesome for icons and Google Fonts for typography. These services may collect anonymous usage data according to their respective privacy policies.

Your Rights

As we don't collect personal data, there's no need to request data deletion or access. Your privacy is inherently protected by our local-only approach.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@mindfulbreak.app
